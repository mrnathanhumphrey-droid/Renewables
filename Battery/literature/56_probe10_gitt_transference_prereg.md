## 0. Framework parentage

RMD-SRC **Step 4a operator-set expansion** (same step-class as Probe 9), but a **fundamentally different operator CLASS**: finite-amplitude time-domain GITT (large-signal) vs small-signal frequency-domain EIS. This is the test lit/53 flagged: does a large-signal concentration-overpotential operator — which linearized EIS cannot see at any frequency — recover transference where the entire EIS lane (10 mHz, low-SoC, sub-mHz) returned NULL?

If YES (TRANSFERENCE RECOVERED): operator-CLASS extension to the C3 amendment (separate amendment doc).
If NO (STILL NULL): transference is confirmed irrecoverable by **any** operator class tested (EIS small-signal + GITT large-signal). The limiting factor is the cohort's design-variation-to-transference-signal ratio, not the measurement modality — a substrate-level statement, not an operator-level one.

## 0.1 Motivating evidence

**GITT smoke (1 nominal cell, fresh, 3 transference levels {0.20, 0.2594, 0.32}, throwaway):** `eta_conc` (concentration polarization at the end of a 1C / 10-min pulse) showed **5.57% spread, monotonic** (226→220→214 mV as t+ rises), ~2× the best EIS operator (1 mHz, 2.73%), and physically correct (higher cation transference → less anion buildup → smaller electrolyte gradient → smaller concentration overpotential). The instantaneous/ohmic part `eta_inst` was dead flat (0.00%) — the right control behavior. This is the finite-amplitude signal lit/53 predicted EIS was structurally blind to.

**Cohort L0 univariate screen (101 cells, zero noise):** one-way ANOVA on raw `eta_conc` across transference levels gives **F=0.08, p=0.92** — the 5.57% within-cell signal is **swamped** by the eta_conc scatter that thickness / particle-radius / amvf variation produces across the L9 cohort. The monotonic trend survives in the group means (233→229→228 mV) but is tiny relative to nuisance variation. **This is the Probe 9 lesson repeating** (within-cell spread ≠ between-condition separability), and starker — EIS R_diff at least caught transference at L0 via Mahalanobis (F=4.14, 8b); univariate GITT eta_conc does not.

**Why a full probe anyway (not closed on the univariate screen):** Probe 8a showed feature-space is decisive — residuals scored 0/3 at L2 while the fresh+aged stack scored 2/3. A univariate ANOVA on one operator is not the C3 architecture. A transference direction could live in a *combination* of GITT operators that the multivariate z-score → PCA → cosine PERMANOVA isolates. This probe runs that test, closing GITT with the **same rigor and architecture** as the EIS probes.

## 1. Hypotheses (LOCKED)

**H10-main (transference recovered):** The GITT operator stack through the C3 architecture (z-score → PCA-k → unit-vector → cosine PERMANOVA) recovers transference at the L2 PRIMARY voltage-noise level: transference verdict = PASS or WEAK PASS in at least one PCA-k variant.

**H10-null:** Transference remains NULL at L2 (and, given the univariate screen, likely already at L0) even with the finite-amplitude GITT operator stack through the multivariate architecture. Confirms transference irrecoverable by EIS small-signal AND GITT large-signal operator classes — a substrate-level limit (design-variation-to-signal ratio), not a modality limit.

**H10-poscontrol (thickness + particle radius separate — sanity):** The GITT stack MUST separate thickness and particle radius at L0 (both are known-separable from every EIS probe). If it cannot, the GITT operators are uninformative, not specifically transference-blind, and the probe is INVALID.

## 3. Architecture (LOCKED — parallels Probe 9 exactly)

**Primary 6D stack** (the GITT analog of the EIS 6D — capacity / instantaneous-resistance / diffusion-polarization):
  x_c = (fresh_Q, fresh_eta_inst, fresh_eta_conc, aged_Q, aged_eta_inst, aged_eta_conc) ∈ ℝ⁶

**Secondary 8D stack** (adds the slow relaxation tail):
  x_c⁺ = x_c ⊕ (fresh_dV_slow, aged_dV_slow) ∈ ℝ⁸

Pipeline (identical to Probe 9 §3): center → z-score by pooled cohort SD → PCA-k (k ∈ {2,3,4}) → unit-vector on the k-sphere → cosine PERMANOVA per design parameter (10K perms, Bonferroni α/3 = 0.0167, same PASS thresholds: F>3.0 & p<0.0167 PASS; F>2.0 & 0.0167≤p<0.05 WEAK).

Run for both stacks × 3 PCA-k × 3 design params × 5 voltage-noise levels. Headline = transference verdict at L2 PRIMARY for the (stack, PCA-k) that maximizes transference F.

## 3.1 Voltage-noise grid (LOCKED — set from cycler precision, independent of the signal)

GITT operators are **voltages** (mV-scale overpotentials), not impedances. Battery-cycler voltage measurement accuracy is ~±0.02–0.1% of full scale (research-grade Arbin/BioLogic/Maccor); derived overpotentials (differences of ~3.5–3.9 V readings) plus SOC/OCV-alignment uncertainty inflate the effective error on a ~100–300 mV operator to roughly the sub-percent range. The grid below is anchored to that physics and is **deliberately much tighter than the EIS grid (σ 15–20%)** — because voltage is *measured* precisely whereas impedance is *fit* noisily. This is the legitimate physical reason GITT could outperform EIS even with a smaller within-cell signal.

| Level | σ (multiplicative, per operator) | mV-equiv on ~220 mV eta_conc | Regime |
|---|---|---|---|
| L0 | 0.00% | 0 | clean baseline |
| L1 | 0.25% | ~0.55 mV | best research lab |
| **L2 (PRIMARY)** | **0.50%** | **~1.1 mV** | typical academic |
| L3 | 1.00% | ~2.2 mV | field |
| L4 | 2.00% | ~4.4 mV | poor instrument |

Multiplicative for parallelism with Probe 9; the mV-equivalent column shows it is physically realistic as additive voltage error too. **Set before any noised PERMANOVA runs; not tuned to the L0 separation screen.** Noise injected per operator with the Probe-9 seed convention (independent fresh/aged channels).

**Note on what the grid can and cannot rescue:** the L0 univariate screen already shows transference swamped by *design* variation (not instrument noise). If transference is NULL in the multivariate stack at **L0**, it is dead regardless of the noise grid — noise only subtracts. The grid governs robustness only if L0 separates.

**P-Probe10_F1 ↔ RMD_F1 (positive control / cleanness):** thickness AND particle radius must PASS in the GITT stack at L0 (both known-separable). If either fails at L0, the GITT operators are uninformative → **PROBE 10 INVALID**, not "transference-blind."

**P-Probe10_F2 ↔ RMD_F2 (PCA-k + stack coherence):** transference verdict must not oscillate non-monotonically across PCA-k {2,3,4} or between the 6D/8D stacks (PASS at one k, NULL at the next, PASS again). Oscillation ⇒ operator instability, documented INCOHERENT.

**P-Probe10_F3 ↔ RMD_F3 (no regression under noise):** thickness + particle radius must remain PASS through at least L2 in the GITT stack (sanity that the noise grid is not destroying all signal).

**P-Probe10_F4 ↔ RMD_F4 (predictive transfer):** N/A — synthetic-only. Khan has no GITT data (academic EIS-only cohort), so no real-cell holdout is possible even on success. Any RECOVERED result would be a synthetic-only finding pending GITT-capable real-cell data.

## 6. What Probe 10 does NOT establish

- No change to lit/47 unless RECOVERED (then a separate operator-class amendment doc).
